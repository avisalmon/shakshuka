How this collection came together

The Project

This is a curated collection of 24 shakshuka recipes, extracted from comprehensive research covering Israel's top chefs, home cooks, and food writers.

Every recipe, technique, and historical insight comes from real Israeli food sources — nothing invented.

The site was built as a love letter to Israel's most beloved breakfast dish.

📊

The Research

Deep analysis of 22+ Israeli recipes, cross-referencing techniques, ingredients, and expert tips across sources.

👨‍🍳

The Chefs

Recipes from Eyal Shani, Rafi Cohen, Bino Gabso, Moshik Roth, Orly Peli-Bronstein, Shay-Lee Lipa, Racheli Krut, Sarit Atar, and many more.

🔬

The Method

Cross-recipe analysis synthesized an "optimal" recipe combining the best techniques from all 22 sources.
